About

Zimbabwe Peace and Development Initiative (ZPDI) is a grass-roots drive, a political, and nonprofit making organisation established in 2016 and formally registered in 2017. We are formed to facilitate sustainable peace and development initiatives amongst women and youth in Zimbabwe. We have always embarked on a humanitarian approach. We believe in serving humanity through advocating for peace as the cornerstone of development. We believe in socio-economic and cultural based decision making spaces for peace and sustainable growth.

  2. Our Programmes

1 Ruzivo Programme / Vocational Training Centres

We envision to provide Vocational Training Centres. It is our obligation to improve the livelihoods of women and vulnerable Youths. Our Vulnerable Youths refer to those out of School but without any adequate life skills to generate income for survival. Likewise women are the most to suffer in economic hardships, most of their husbands are informally employed, therefor improving the income generating capacity of the women is aligned to our core aim of development.

2 Pamuhacha Peace Building Programme

Our Peace Dialogues revolve around the areas of Politics and Gender development. Political and Gender conflict has been the major challenge in our Zimbabwean context. Through peace dialogues we seek to ensure a pacific approach in conflict management. We believe that disagreements and conflicts can always be discussed through arbitration.

3 Mabasa Ebudiriro / Income Grenerating Projects

We believe in sustainable development goals and fundamental rights enshrined in the Convention of Human Rights Charter of 1940. In this case we believe it is the right of every vulnerable youth and Women to food, shelter and health and the best way is coming up with creative ways of fighting poverty through income generating projects.

4 Tambanudza Ruoko Programme / Reachout Poverty Reduction

This is a program aimed at assisting the less privileged people; elderly people, people with disabilities, children and women with food, clothing and shelter security. As ZPDI we engage various partners including both international, local and government in fundraising for basic human needs including food, shelter and clothing. In the case we seek to address fundamental issues that destabilize peace in our country from community level.

Poverty and vulnerability is one of the major challenges. Through this programme our peace and development efforts agenda is properly aligned to our humanitarian pledge.

ZPDI

Zimbabwe Peace and Development Initiative seeks to promote democracy and rule of law using the principle of peace, training and entrepreneurship, and unity thereby fostering harmony and mutual cooperation among all Zimbabweans, Youths and Women in particular.
