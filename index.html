Welcome to ZPDI

(Zimbabwe Peace and Development Initiative)

Contact us

Zimbabwe Peace and Development Initiative (ZPDI) is a grass-roots drive, a political, and nonprofit making organisation established in 2016 and formally registered in 2017.

We are formed to facilitate sustainable peace and development initiatives amongst women and youth in Zimbabwe. We have always embarked on a humanitarian approach.

  • Our Target Communities: Urban, Peri Urban, Rural, Farm Settlements.
  • Our Target Groups: Women, Vulnerable youths, Religious groups, People with disabilities, Elderly.
  • Core Values: Confidentiality, Pro-activeness, Measurement, Collaboration, Nurturing.

We believe in serving humanity through advocating for peace as the cornerstone of development. We believe in socio-economic and cultural based decision making spaces for peace and sustainable growth.

Values

Our Vision

To be a bearer of Peace and Development in Zimbabwean Communities.

Aim

To generate various stakeholders in the process of peace and development

Mission
  • To improve the livelihoods of women and vulnerable youths through establishment of vocational centres.
  • To facilitate skills capacity building in income generating projects for vulnerable youths and women.
  • To engage various stakeholders in peace dialogues.
Our Services
  • Vocational Training Centres
  • Peace Dialogues (Political, Gender, Based)
  • Income generating Projects
  • Poverty reduction/ reach-out

ZPDI

Zimbabwe Peace and Development Initiative seeks to promote democracy and rule of law using the principle of peace, training and entrepreneurship, and unity thereby fostering harmony and mutual cooperation among all Zimbabweans, Youths and Women in particular.
